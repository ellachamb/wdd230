Target Audience

The target audience for this website includes many different individuals and businesses wanting to know more about the Cedar Hills Chamber of Commerce and the offerings of the city. It is for people who live in and out of Cedar Hills wanting to know more about the city, as well as informing about events and news. It is also for businesses who are looking into starting in Cedar Hills. They can get an idea of the potential of the city.

Personas

Number 1

Name: Lori Daniels

Lori Daniels is the co-owner of a small locally owned business seeing desserts. They have been a business for 4 years now, and she is wanting to start expanding her business to a different area. She heard that Cedar Hills was a small city, but was growing exponentially with many houses being built. She is visiting this website because she is interested in what businesses Cedar Hills already has and if the city is a good fit for her small dessert business.

Number 2

Samuel Christensen

Samuel Christensen is currently living in Cedar Hills and is interested in joining the chamber of commerce. By going on the website he will be able to see their mission, current members, and also look at testimonials from members. He will also be able to apply to get on the chamber of commerce, and see the benefits of being a member.
